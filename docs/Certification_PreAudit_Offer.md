# ✅ Certification Pre-Audit Screening Offer

**Komba GIS AB – Spatial Compliance Partner Network**

---

## 1. Purpose

Provide a rapid, data-driven pre-audit assessment for clients preparing for FSC, PEFC, or carbon standard verification. The service blends geospatial risk screening, document review, and partner-led site assessments to highlight compliance gaps and corrective action priorities before formal audits.

---

## 2. Scope of Standards

- **Forest Stewardship Council (FSC):** Supply-chain and forest management checks against Principles & Criteria, with focus on traceability, conservation values, and worker rights.
- **Programme for the Endorsement of Forest Certification (PEFC):** Assessment of sustainable management requirements, including harvesting practices, biodiversity safeguards, and stakeholder engagement.
- **Carbon Standards (e.g., VCS, Gold Standard):** Review of MRV plans, leakage management, permanence risk, and alignment with emission reduction methodologies.

---

## 3. Assessment Workflow

1. **Pre-Audit Intake:** Collect existing management plans, audit reports, and geospatial data; identify target standards and operational boundaries.
2. **Spatial & Document Review:** Run EUDR-aligned spatial checks (deforestation, protected areas, land-use consistency) and evaluate documentation against standard-specific criteria.
3. **Gap Analysis & Corrective Actions:** Classify compliance status (Compliant / Needs Improvement / Critical) for each requirement; propose corrective measures and responsible parties.
4. **Action Planning Workshop:** Align with client and partners on remediation roadmap, timelines, and evidence requirements for formal audit submission.

---

## 4. Deliverables

- Gap assessment matrix mapping requirements to evidence, status, and priority.
- Corrective action plan with timelines, resource needs, and verification methods.
- Portfolio of spatial exhibits (maps, Excel summaries) supporting compliance claims.
- Optional on-site verification summary (in partnership with forestry auditors or drone teams).
- Briefing pack for audit firms detailing preparatory work and outstanding items.

---

## 5. Team & Partnerships

- **Komba GIS AB:** Spatial risk screening, data integration, and reporting.
- **Certification Specialists:** FSC/PEFC auditors or consultants providing standards interpretation and documentation review.
- **Field & Drone Teams:** Support targeted inspections and photographic evidence collection.
