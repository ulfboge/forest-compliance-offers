 ## 1. Objective

 Support companies in meeting EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR, Reg. (EU) 2023/1115) requirements by providing:

 - **Full spatial traceability** of commodity origin
 - **Evidence-based risk assessment** using satellite data and forest monitoring
 - **Expert forestry interpretation** and reporting aligned with EU due diligence workflows

 ## 3. Methodology Overview

 1. **Data Collection** ‚Äì Supplier coordinates, boundary files, and supporting documentation.
 2. **Data Cleaning & Validation** ‚Äì Coordinate accuracy, projection standardization (EPSG:4326 / UTM).
 3. **Remote Sensing Analysis** ‚Äì Multi-temporal forest-loss assessment (pre/post-2020 baseline).
 4. **Spatial Risk Overlay** ‚Äì Combine results with land-use designations, protected areas, slope, and governance indicators.
 5. **Verification** ‚Äì On-ground or drone-based validation for uncertain cases.
 6. **Reporting & Integration** ‚Äì Produce outputs compatible with the EU EUDR Information System and partner dashboards.
