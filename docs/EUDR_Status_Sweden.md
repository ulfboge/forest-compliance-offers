 ## 3. Transitional Arrangements & Exemptions

 - **Pre-December 2025 Timber:** Timber harvested before **30 December 2025** and placed on the market before that date is exempt from EUDR obligations, provided operators can demonstrate harvest date through existing felling notifications.
 - **Smallholders:** Micro and small Swedish forest owners benefit from the delayed obligation date (June 2026) but must still prepare geolocation data.
 - **Low-Risk Country Simplifications:** Operators sourcing exclusively from Swedish forests (low-risk) may apply simplified due diligence but must still submit geolocation polygons and risk assessments.
