## 1. Overview

We extend the EUDR spatial compliance service with financial and operational planning that helps forest owners, cooperatives, and investors quantify asset value, plan infrastructure, and align management actions with long-term revenue goals. The offer combines forest inventory analytics, cash-flow modeling, and scenario planning for both timber and non-timber forest products (NTFPs).

## 3. Deliverables

- Forest asset valuation report with DCF tables and sensitivity plots.
- Investment plan (capex/opex) mapped to operational milestones.
- Scenario workbook (Excel) for timber/NTFP production mixes.
- Risk and financing memo aligned with sustainability-linked criteria.
- Optional integration with EUDR traceability outputs for coherent storytelling.

---

## 4. Data Requirements

- Forest inventory data (species composition, standing volume, age classes).
- Historical production records and local price benchmarks.
- Infrastructure and equipment inventory (machinery, roads, storage, logistics).
- Operating cost structure (labor, fuel, maintenance, compliance fees).
- Policy and certification requirements relevant to the target market.
